The 3 photos we need

WideFull site, crew, equipment, surroundings.
MediumCrew, materials, equipment, active work.
Close-upClean detail that explains the work.

Retake the photo if

  • It is blurry, too dark, or taken through a dirty window.
  • The subject is cut off, blocked, too close, or hard to understand.
  • It does not clearly show people, equipment, work, or finished result.
