The 3 photos we need

1
Wide Show the full site context, crew scale, equipment, and surroundings.
2
Medium Show the crew, equipment, materials, or active work from a clear useful angle.
3
Close-up Show a clean detail that makes the work look professional and easy to understand.

Retake the photo if

  • It is blurry when you zoom in.
  • It is too dark to see the work clearly.
  • The photo was taken through a dirty windshield or window.
  • The main subject is cut off or too close to understand.
  • Fingers, tools, glare, or equipment block the view.
  • The photo does not clearly show the people, equipment, work, or finished result.

Simple rule: if the photo cannot support a campaign, LinkedIn post, or project story, retake it before leaving.

One clear retake on site is faster than trying to fix a blurry, dark, or unusable photo later.
